2Structure

Support

  • NO posts in ground · Freestanding on pedestals
  • 19× ECO M (1.4"–2.6") + 19× ECO L (2.6"–5.1")
  • Base: flat stone pavers, compacted gravel, or combo
  • 1–2 precast concrete bases at corners (optional)

Framing

  • NO doubled beam · Joists sit directly on pedestals/stone
  • Doubled 2×6 rims all around (two boards face-screwed)
  • Main deck: 2×6 joists, 16" O.C., 11.5' full length, front-to-back
  • Curved edge: 2×4 joists fanning from ledger toward pool
  • Ledger: 2×4 at transition line, connects curved to main
  • Border sleeper: 2×6 × 22' under perpendicular back border

Decking Direction

  • Main field (12' wide): Runs left→right (parallel to back edge)
  • Back border (1' wide): Runs perpendicular (front-to-back) · Break Line
  • Curved section: Runs perpendicular (radial/front-to-back)
  • Transition: 2×4 ledger + border sleeper create clean break lines

4Stain Checklist

1
Pressure wash all boards · Let dry 48+ hrs
2
Label every piece with position (e.g., "DECK-ROW-3") · Painter's tape + marker
3
End grain first: Heavy coat on all cut ends · Double coat · Let dry 30 min
4
Roll faces: 9" roller, ¼" nap · Work in sections · Maintain wet edge
5
Back-brush: 3" quality brush · Work stain into cracks · Hand-finished look
6
Flip & repeat: Dry 4–6 hrs, flip, stain back faces and edges
7
Second coat (optional): After 24 hrs · Two thin > one heavy
💡 Morning shade = friend · Hot sun dries stain too fast · Work early or in shade

6Build Order

1
Site Prep: Clear · Grade ±1" · Stone/gravel base · Layout pedestals
2
Stain: All 6 faces before assembly · 2–3 days · Dry weather
3
Main Frame: Doubled 2×6 rims · 9 field joists 16" O.C. · On pedestals · Level to ~7"
4
Blocking + Sleeper: Mid-span blocking · 2×6 sleeper (22') for border support
5
Ledger: Install 2×4 at transition line · Screwed to rim/blocking
6
Curved Frame: 2×4 joists from ledger toward pool · ~5" total · Overhang coping ~1"
7
Main Decking: 5/4×6 left→right · ¼" gaps · 2.5" screws · Two per joist
8
Border: Perpendicular 1' pieces along back edge · Break Line accent
9
Curved Decking: 2×6 radial/front-to-back · Follow curve · Cut to match rim
10
Railings + Step: 6×6 posts · 2×6 rails · 36" height · Box step · 7" rise

11Reminders
