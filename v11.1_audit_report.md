# v11.1 Picture-Frame Design Audit Report

**Project:** pool-deck-plans  
**Audit Date:** May 17, 2026  
**Current Version:** v11.0  
**Target Version:** v11.1 (Picture-Frame Double Borders)

---

## Executive Summary

The transition from v11.0 to v11.1 represents a **fundamental redesign** of the deck structure. Every documentation file requires substantial updates — not just version bumps — because the entire geometry, framing, materials, and construction sequence have changed.

**v11.0 Design:** Single 22' × ~18' deck with one 1' back-edge "Break Line" border and a curved pool section.

**v11.1 Design:** Two 11' × 11.5' sections with **double-row picture-frame borders on all four sides** of each section, a 6" center seam between sections, no curved pool-edge section (or it attaches separately), and inner field dimensions of 9' 2" × 9' 6".
